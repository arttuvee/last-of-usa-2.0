Day usage

You have the option to investigate either one large airport or two medium-sized airports per day.


If you choose to explore two medium-sized airports, you will need to select a medium-sized airport to fly to first.
Once you have selected a medium-sized airport, you will only be able to fly to other medium-sized airports within range for the remainder of the day.
